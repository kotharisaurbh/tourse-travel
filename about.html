about train booking

For booking train tickets from sktrip, enter the name of the city, station name or station code of the cities you will depart from and arrive at. From the updated list of trains available, choose your preferred class of travel. While making the railway reservation on our app or website, you would need to add your IRCTC username. This verification process is important. It makes sure that your IRCTC reservation does not fail once you have paid for the ticket. If you don't have a username, you can easily create one by adding the details required.
